What kind of games do I make?

I mostly like to make 2D games the are goofy and funny, I also like to make a lot of retro and pixel art games. My games are usually all-ages but some of them are 12+ and are not suitable for everyone.


What do I use to make my games?

My first games were made in Scratch, which is a kids coding language for learning to code, but my later games use PenguinMod, which is an advanced mod of Scratch, which allows to much more stuff. I also use Godot, which uses a language called GDscript.


What other programming languages do I know?

Besides Scratch and GDscript, I also know some Python, and I am planning to learn some other languages in the future, like JavaScript and more. (I also know some HTML and CSS, but those are technically not programming languages!)


Am I good at game development and programming??

I love game development and programming, and my games are decent and fun, but I still want to improve. Also, the kind of games that I like to make do not require a huge amount of skill, but I still want learn how to do slightly more complex things in the future. I also sometimes like to make games that require things I have never done before.
